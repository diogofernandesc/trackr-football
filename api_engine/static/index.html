FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What competitions does your API cover?

Currently, Trackr.Football only supports the Premier League, but there is future plans for more competitions.

Where do you get your data from?

Currently the main chunk of the data comes from the open FPL endpoints and some other open football APIs, which I've cleaned up, aggregated (where applicable) in order to make more sense of the data.

What does player influence mean?

This is a new endpoint I am currently working on, player influence comes down to the social media influence of players; how much they are talked about and general public sentiment.
